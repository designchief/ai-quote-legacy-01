Assumptions

Data protection

Collection and use of your personal information

Consent. By arranging this insurance, you consented to all your information being used as outlined below, including specific consent for sensitive information such as medical details or convictions. If you provide any information about someone else (e.g. an additional insured), you must have this person's consent and have made the person aware of the terms of this insurance.

Use of your information. We may use your personal information, including sensitive data, for insurance administration purposes, including underwriting, claims handling and re-insurance. We may also share your details with your intermediary, any agent authorised by you to act on your behalf, the Allianz Group and regulatory bodies. To help us check information provided and also to prevent fraudulent claims we may also share information with the Irish Insurance Federation's Insurance Link Anti-Fraud register and with private investigators when we need to investigate a claim.

We will hold your information only for the time necessary for insurance administration purposes.

In order for us to keep your information up to date, you should contact Allianz or your insurance intermediary if any of your details change.

Access. You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal information held by Allianz. To do so, please write to: The Data Protection Officer, Allianz House, Elmpark, Merrion Road, Dublin 4. A fee of €6.35 is chargeable and cheques should be made payable to Allianz.

Call recording. Calls may be recorded or monitored for regulatory, training and quality purposes.

Direct marketing. We may keep you informed by post, telephone, e-mail, SMS or other means about our products and services and may share your details with Allianz Group members for marketing purposes.
